Tentative Course Outline

  • ■ The online course is a 10-day workshop with 2-3 hours of live session everyday, which comes to a total of 30 hours.
  • ■ The course will cover topics from classical computing to quantum computing, Qiskit programming, and finally conventional quantum algorithms such as the Deutsch-Jozsa algorithm, Grover's algorithm, and Simon's algorithm.
  • ■ Each session will be recorded and shared to the participants during the training phase, so that the students can review them at their own pace.
  • ■ There will be assessments after each session. Participants have to earn at least 50% on each assessment and have an overall grade > 70% to receive a certificate from QWorld. The commitments of the participants to attend all the training programs and the assessment results will be the selection criteria for the "Team-building" phase.
